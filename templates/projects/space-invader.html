Space Invader

Space Invader is a classic-style arcade game built with Python and Pygame. Players control a spaceship to shoot down waves of aliens while dodging incoming threats. With limited lives and increasing speed each round, the game challenges players to survive and earn the highest score possible.

  • Wave Progression - New waves of aliens appear after clearing the current one, increasing difficulty.
  • Score Tracking - Displays and updates the player's score in real time as aliens are destroyed.
  • Life System - Players start with 3 lives and lose one if hit by an alien or if aliens reach the bottom.
  • Dynamic Speed - Alien, bullet, and ship speeds increase with each round to raise challenge.
  • Restart Mechanism - A “Play” button appears after game over, allowing players to restart anytime.
